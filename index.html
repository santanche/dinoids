Welcome to Dinoids

  • Press [Play] to start the game.
  • Press [Step] to go forward step by step.
  • Press [*] to launch a bomb in order to reorganize dinoids.

Start the Game

Your goal is to put all dinoids in the same direction in a minimal number of steps.
